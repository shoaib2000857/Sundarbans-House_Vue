Guidelines
Community Rules
Keep our spaces healthy and welcoming for everyone
01
Be Respectful
Treat all members with dignity. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks of any kind.
02
Stay On Topic
Keep messages relevant to the group's purpose. Use the right group for the right conversation.
03
No Spam or Promotions
Do not send unsolicited promotional content, chain messages, or excessive forwarding.
04
Protect Privacy
Never share personal data of members outside the group without explicit consent.
05
Admins Have Final Say
House reps and admins maintain group health. Violations may result in removal without warning.
06
Report Concerns
If you see rule violations or need help, DM a house rep or use the anonymous confession wall.
